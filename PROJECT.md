Project: Playful Math for Kids

Goal
----
Create a safe, accessible, and playful website where children (ages 8–10) learn core math concepts through short, scaffolded mini-games that adapt to the child’s level and encourage repeated practice.

Target users
------------
- Primary: Children aged 8–10 who are learning core arithmetic, problem-solving, and number sense.
- Secondary: Parents and caregivers who want progress visibility and safe, constructive feedback for their children.
- Tertiary: Teachers and tutors who may assign or recommend games for practice.

Constraints
-----------
- Accessibility: WCAG AA where practical (readable fonts, keyboard support, color contrast, screen-reader labels).
- Privacy & Safety: COPPA-aware design; minimal data collection; parental consent required for account creation and data storage.
- Platforms: Responsive web app targeting modern desktop and tablet browsers first, then mobile.
- Performance: Games must load in under 3 seconds on typical home broadband; assets should be optimized for low-memory devices.
- Content: Curriculum-aligned, age-appropriate math tasks; no ads or third-party tracking in children's flows.
- Tech stack (suggested): Static frontend (React/Vite or similar), lightweight backend for auth and progress (optional), analytics with privacy controls.
